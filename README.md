## English

### 🎯 Overview

**MechDesignCopilot** is the first AI skill specifically designed for mechanical engineering. It provides comprehensive guidance from conceptual design to manufacturing documentation, covering structural design, parametric calculations, 3D modeling, BOM generation, and mechatronics integration.

Whether you're a student, engineer, or expert, this assistant adapts to your level and guides you through the complete design process.

---

### ✨ Key Features

#### 🔄 **Full-Process Guidance**
- **Requirements Analysis** → Define functional needs and performance specs
- **Conceptual Design** → Generate and compare multiple schemes
- **Detailed Calculations** → Strength verification, kinematic analysis, life assessment
- **3D Modeling & Drawings** → Part modeling, assembly design, engineering drawings
- **BOM & Manufacturing** → Generate BOM lists, cost estimation, supplier info

#### 💬 **Confirmation-Driven Interaction**
Not a "one-click generator" — it's a **design partner**. Every step confirms with you:
- ✓ Requirements understanding correct?
- ✓ Design scheme satisfied?
- ✓ Calculation parameters reasonable?

No more AI "guessing" — you stay in control.

#### 📊 **Layered Output for All Levels**
- **Students**: Detailed principles, formula derivations, step-by-step guidance
- **Engineers**: Quick data lookup, formulas, standards — concise and practical
- **Experts**: Deep verification, multi-scheme comparison, compliance checks

#### 🔧 **Mechatronics Integration**
- Mechanical structural design
- Circuit design (sensors, actuators, controllers)
- Component selection and procurement guidance
- Software recommendations (CAD, CAE, PCB tools)

## 中文

### 🎯 概述

**MechDesignCopilot**（机械设计副驾驶）是首个专为机械工程领域打造的AI助手。它提供从概念设计到制造文档的全流程指导，涵盖结构设计、参数计算、3D建模、BOM清单生成及机电一体化集成。

无论您是学生、工程师还是专家，这个助手都会根据您的水平，引导您完成整个设计过程。

---

### ✨ 核心特性

#### 🔄 **全流程指导**
- **需求分析** → 定义功能需求与性能指标
- **方案设计** → 生成并对比多个方案
- **详细计算** → 强度校核、运动学分析、寿命评估
- **3D建模出图** → 零件建模、装配设计、工程图绘制
- **BOM与制造** → 生成BOM清单、成本估算、供应商信息

#### 💬 **确认式交互**
不是"一键生成"，而是**设计伙伴**。每一步都与您确认：
- ✓ 需求理解对吗？
- ✓ 方案满意吗？
- ✓ 计算参数合理吗？

不再有AI"自作聪明"——您掌控全局。

#### 📊 **分层输出，适配全用户**
- **学生**：详细讲解原理、公式推导、逐步引导
- **工程师**：快速查询数据、公式、标准——简洁实用
- **专家**：深入验证、多方案对比、合规性检查

#### 🔧 **机电一体化融合**
- 机械结构设计
- 电路设计（传感器、执行器、控制器）
- 元器件选型与采购指导
- 软件推荐（CAD、CAE、电路设计工具）
